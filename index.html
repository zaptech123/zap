Zap tech is a new innovative technology that allows people to charge an external battery for their phone simply through movement. We have designed comfortable foot inserts containing Piezoelectric discs that convert the pressure created by your feet when moving into electrical energy that gets stored up in a flexible external battery that fits perfectly in the side of almost any shoe.
